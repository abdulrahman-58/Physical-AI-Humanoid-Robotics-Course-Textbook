# Intel RealSense D435i 2025 Pricing Research

<DifficultyIndicator level="intermediate" />

This document provides detailed research on the Intel RealSense D435i camera pricing and availability for 2025. The RealSense D435i is the primary depth camera recommended for the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics course, providing essential perception capabilities for robot navigation and interaction.

## Product Overview

### Intel RealSense D435i Depth Camera
- **Product Code**: Intel RealSense D435i
- **Release Date**: Launched in 2019, mature product in 2025
- **Target Market**: Robotics, AR/VR, 3D scanning, research
- **Technology**: Stereo vision depth sensing with integrated IMU

## Technical Specifications

### Key Specifications
- **Depth Technology**: Active stereo vision
- **Depth Stream**: 1280×720 at 30/60/90 FPS
- **RGB Stream**: 1920×1080 at 30 FPS
- **Field of View**: 87° × 58° × 94° (H×V×D) ±5°
- **Minimum Depth Distance**: 0.2m (0.1m at 30fps)
- **Maximum Range**: 10m (typical)
- **Accuracy**: ±2% at 1m distance
- **Connectivity**: USB 3.0 Type-C
- **Integrated IMU**: Gyroscope and accelerometer
- **Operating Temperature**: 0°C to 40°C
- **Power Consumption**: 1.6W typical, 2.0W max
- **Weight**: 57g (without cable)

### Advanced Features
- **Active IR Stereo**: Uses IR illumination for enhanced depth sensing
- **IMU Integration**: Built-in gyroscope and accelerometer for motion compensation
- **Flexible Mounting**: Multiple mounting options with standard threading
- **Wide Compatibility**: Works with ROS 2, Isaac Sim, OpenCV, and other frameworks
