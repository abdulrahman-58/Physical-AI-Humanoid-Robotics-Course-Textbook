# Unitree G1 2025 Pricing Research

<DifficultyIndicator level="intermediate" />

This document provides detailed research on the Unitree G1 humanoid robot pricing and availability for 2025. The Unitree G1 is the primary humanoid robot platform recommended for the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics course.

## Product Overview

### Unitree G1 Humanoid Robot
- **Product Code**: Unitree G1 Humanoid Robot
- **Release Date**: Officially announced in 2024, shipping began in late 2024, widely available in 2025
- **Target Market**: Education, research, development
- **Design Focus**: Affordable humanoid platform for research and education

## Technical Specifications

### Key Specifications
- **Degrees of Freedom**: 32 DOF (19 in legs, 6 in arms, 7 in torso/head)
- **Dimensions**: 1.1m height, 13.2kg weight
- **Actuators**: 32 custom high-performance servo actuators
- **Sensors**: Multiple IMUs, force/torque sensors, cameras
- **Battery**: 25.9V/6.6Ah lithium battery (1 hour operation)
- **Max Speed**: 3.2 m/s running, 1.2 m/s walking
- **Payload**: 10kg maximum payload
- **Connectivity**: Wi-Fi, Ethernet
- **Operating System**: Real-time OS with ROS 2 support
- **Development**: SDK with C++ and Python APIs
