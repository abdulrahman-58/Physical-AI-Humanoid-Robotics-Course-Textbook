# Jetson Orin Nano Super 2025 Pricing Research

<DifficultyIndicator level="intermediate" />

This document provides detailed research on the Jetson Orin Nano Super pricing and availability for 2025. The Jetson Orin Nano Super is the primary computing platform recommended for the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics course.

## Product Overview

### Jetson Orin Nano Super Developer Kit
- **Product Code**: Jetson Orin Nano Super Developer Kit
- **Release Date**: Officially released in 2023, widely available in 2025
- **Target Market**: Education, robotics, edge AI applications
- **Architecture**: NVIDIA Orin SoC (System on Chip)

## Technical Specifications

### Key Specifications
- **CPU**: 6-core ARM Cortex-A78AE (ARM v8.2) 64-bit processor @ 2.0 GHz
- **GPU**: 1024-core NVIDIA Ampere architecture GPU
- **Memory**: 8GB LPDDR5 memory (102.4 GB/s)
- **Storage**: 32GB eMMC 5.1
- **Power**: 15W typical, 25W maximum (with optional power boost)
- **Connectivity**:
  - Dual Gigabit Ethernet
  - Wi-Fi 6/6E (2.4GHz + 5GHz + 6GHz)
  - Bluetooth 5.2
- **Expansion**:
  - 40-pin GPIO header (Raspberry Pi compatible)
  - 2x MIPI CSI-2 camera connectors
  - 1x M.2 Key E slot (PCIe Gen4 x1 + USB 3.2 Gen1)
  - 1x M.2 Key M slot (PCIe Gen4 x2)
